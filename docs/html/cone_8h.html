Performs u = u + (1 + a) * e where e is the cannonical vector for each cone LP Cone: e = ones(n), second-order cone: e = (1,0,0,...) and a is the minimum scalar value such that u + (1 + a) * e is in cone C.

Parameters
uVector to bring to cone.
soc_idxArray pointing to the start of each SOC block. Only used in the GPU (cone.cu) implementation.

zOutput vector.
lDimension of LP cone.

qArray of second-order cone dimensions.
